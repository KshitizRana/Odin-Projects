Tea recipe

Description

ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Tea Leaves
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Put a tea pan on the inducton.
  2. Add 1/2 cup of water in it.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of tea leaf and sugar.
  4. Let it boil for 2 minutes.
  5. Add 1 cup milk
  6. Let it boil for 3-5 minutes
  7. Serve it
